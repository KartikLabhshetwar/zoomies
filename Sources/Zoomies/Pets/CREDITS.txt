Pet sprite credits
==================

These pet animations come from the webpets project
(https://github.com/sankalpaacharya/webpets). The majority of the assets
originate from vscode-pets (https://github.com/tonybaloney/vscode-pets).

Zoomies ships only the creatures that walk on legs. Artists for those:
- Dog animations — NVPH Studio (https://nvph-studio.itch.io/dog-animation-4-different-dogs)
- Akita inu — Kevin Huang (https://github.com/kevin2huang)
- Fox — Elthen (https://twitter.com/pixelthen)
- Turtle — enkeefe (via https://www.pixilart.com/draw)
- Horse — adapted by Chris Kent (https://github.com/thechriskent) from
  assets by Onfe (https://onfe.itch.io/horse-sprite-with-rider-asset-pack)
- Panda — Jessie Ferris (https://github.com/jeferris)
- Skeleton — adapted by Chris Kent from assets by MonoPixelArt
  (https://monopixelart.itch.io/)

Deno, rat, and vampire assets are also from the webpets / vscode-pets sets;
see those repositories for full licensing details. The dog assets ship an
explicit license at dog/license.txt.

Classic sprite-sheet pets (from Zoomies 1.0):
- Cat — the oneko.js sprite by adryd (https://github.com/adryd325/oneko.js), MIT.
  License: cat/license.txt.
- Dalmatian — a Neko Archive sprite, public domain (GOODCLOVER).
  License: dalmatian/license.txt.
